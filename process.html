Process

A calm, collaborative onboarding experience from day one.

Harper Ledger delivers a structured, transparent process so you always know what happens next. From intake to your first close, every milestone is documented and communicated.

Onboarding timeline

Most clients complete onboarding within 2-3 weeks, depending on the state of their books.

Step 1: Discovery + diagnostics

Review your financial stack, access existing files, and outline what needs cleanup or catching up.

Step 2: Cleanup + catch-up

Reconcile past periods, fix categorization issues, and confirm a clean starting point.

Step 3: Workflow setup

Implement monthly close checklists, approval workflows, and reporting dashboards.

Step 4: Monthly rhythm

Receive monthly reports, a walkthrough call, and action items for the next cycle.

What you can expect

Clear communication

Dedicated Slack channel, shared progress tracker, and a 48-hour response time.

Documented close

Close checklists and monthly deliverables shared in a secure folder.

Actionable insights

A short list of priorities each month so you know what to focus on next.
