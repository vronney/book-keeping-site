Services

Bookkeeping services that keep your finances clean, compliant, and insightful.

Every service is tailored to your size, industry, and tools. Harper Ledger focuses on monthly bookkeeping, reporting, and advisory so you always know where your business stands.

Core bookkeeping services

These are the essentials that keep your books accurate, tax-ready, and organized all year long.

Monthly reconciliations

Reconcile bank accounts and credit cards to ensure every transaction is captured correctly.

Accounts payable + receivable

Track bills, vendor payments, and customer invoices to improve cash flow visibility.

Financial statements

Deliver monthly P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statements with clear explanations.

Expense categorization

Classify transactions accurately to ensure clean reports and deductible expenses are tracked.

Payroll coordination

Sync payroll providers, map wages and taxes correctly, and reconcile payroll liabilities.

Sales tax + 1099 prep

Track sales tax obligations, prepare 1099 data, and organize documents for your tax team.

Advisory + reporting

Beyond clean books, Harper Ledger provides insight so you can forecast, plan, and lead with clarity.

Cash flow forecasting

Rolling 3-12 month forecasts to track runway, hiring plans, and seasonal swings.

KPI dashboards

Customized dashboards that highlight revenue, margins, burn rate, and client profitability.

Budgeting + scenario planning

Annual budgets and what-if models to support growth, hiring, and investment decisions.

Financial clean-up

Catch-up bookkeeping to repair prior periods and align your chart of accounts.
